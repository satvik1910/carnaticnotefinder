About RagaNoteFinder

What is RagaNoteFinder?

RagaNoteFinder is a powerful web application designed to help musicians, students, and enthusiasts of Indian classical music identify and analyze the notes in Carnatic music performances. Our tool allows you to upload audio or provide a YouTube URL to analyze the notes being played in a performance.

How It Works

  1. Upload an audio file or provide a YouTube URL with timestamps
  2. Our system processes the audio to extract musical notes
  3. View detailed analysis including note sequences, pitch, and timing
  4. Save and share your analyses with others

Features

Note Detection

Accurately identifies Carnatic music notes from audio sources.

YouTube Integration

Analyze music directly from YouTube videos by URL.

Visual Analysis

View pitch and note visualizations for better understanding.

Save & Share

Save your analyses and share them with the community.
